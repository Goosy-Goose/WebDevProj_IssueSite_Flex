Using AI Art

Pros and Cons

Pros:
- Quickly create art without much skill.

- Create wholly unique artwork.

- Costs minimal money.

Cons: (not including ethical issues)

- bby all you did was type words. The words is the art, not the image. (Completion of artwork is not as fulfilling)

- Homogeneous art styles.

- AI generated artwork is not copyrighted.

Recommended Use

A The Hollywood Reporter article articulates that AI generated art is non-copyrightable, and therefore in the public domain for free use.

So please do not sell AI Art. Obviously you could just not tell anyone that the artwork you're selling is AI, but, dipping slightly into ethics, selling free things for money is just kind of scummy :(.

Perhaps consider using AI generated art to inspire your own artwork whether that be visual, audio, or other. AI models are able to reach a much more varied set of references than you and will mash them together in a unique way. AI generated art can provide distinctly new ideas for you to draw from.
