AI Art

What is it?

AI art is any art created using a generative artificial intelligence based on neural networks, deep learning algorithms, and more. Usually, AI art is created using software such as DALL-E 2 or Adobe Firefly to generate artwork based on a written descriptive prompt.

How does it work?

In most cases, these AI art algorithms utilize neural networks to detect patterns in a set of "training data".

The algorithm then takes note of these patterns and generates its own patterns in response to user input.

For instance, an AI program trained on a collection of images of dogs will detect patterns in the images using color, contrast, and metadata to determine that a dog's face usually has two black dots above a third black dot (eyes and nose).

Images of chihuahuas and muffins.

It is unlikely that an AI would mistake a muffin for a chihuahua these days, considering how much AI has advanced, but it is entirely possible that an AI would collect the patterns in these images and group them under the same label.

Examples of AI Art

Prompt: Create a surreal scene featuring giant flowers sprouting from floating islands with an interesting blend of coral reefs, butterflies, and stars. Compose it in a style inspired by Salvador Dali.


Prompt: Create inspirational and uplifting typography art with Peace Train arranged circularly with creative use of fonts, sizes, colors, and styles.
